About Chidan's Crotchet

Post By Admin Likescomments

Chidans Fashion Concept is a standard and registered crotchet clothing & footwear producing company that will be located in WorldBank- Owerri; in an ideal location highly suitable for the kind of business we want to establish. Chidan's will be involved in producing crotchet wears in different forms such as footwear, fashionable bags, Bowlers, babies and adult clothing, house slippers and slipper socks and others.

We are set to services a wide range of clientele not just in Nigeria, but also to all parts of the world.Chidans Fashion Concept will ensure that all the footwear and wears that leave our factory are of the highest quality and highly durable and affordable.
